Cloudiness

A city's latitude is fairly strongly correlated with its maximum temperature, with cities closer to the equator experiencing hotter temperatures. It is interesting to note that cities in the southern hemisphere are warmer on average than those in the nothern hemisphere, perhaps due to the fact that the data may have been gathered when it was summer in the southern hemisphere.
